You're Not Alone

If you or someone you know needs support, these resources are available 24/7. All calls and texts are free and confidential.

National Domestic Violence Hotline

1-800-799-7233 (SAFE)

TTY: 1-800-787-3224

Available 24/7. Trained advocates provide support, safety planning, and referrals to local services in over 200 languages.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Free, 24/7 crisis support via text message. Trained crisis counselors are ready to help.

National Sexual Assault Hotline (RAINN)

1-800-656-4673 (HOPE)

Free, confidential, 24/7 support from trained staff members. Also available via online chat.

National Alliance for Eating Disorders Helpline

1-866-931-7733

Free, confidential support and referrals for anyone affected by eating disorders.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988

Free, confidential support for people in distress. Available 24/7 by phone, text, or online chat.

Safety tip: If you're concerned about someone monitoring your phone or internet activity, consider calling from a friend's phone or a public computer. You can also use your browser's private/incognito mode when visiting support websites.

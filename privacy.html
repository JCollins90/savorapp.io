Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The short version: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect it, we don't see it, and we don't sell it. Period.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information.

Savor does not:

  • Require account creation
  • Collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your location (unless you explicitly enable location features locally on your device)
  • Use analytics or tracking software
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use advertising or ad tracking

All data you enter into the app ‚Äî recipes, notes, journal entries, tracked information ‚Äî is stored only on your device.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using:

  • iOS secure storage (Keychain for sensitive data such as passcodes)
  • On-device database for all content

This means:

  • We cannot access your data
  • We cannot recover your data if it is lost
  • We cannot be compelled to provide your data to third parties because we do not have it

3. Optional Features

Camera Access

If you grant camera permission, photos are stored only on your device. Camera data is never transmitted anywhere. You can revoke camera permission at any time in your device's Settings.

Location Access

If you enable optional location features, location data is stored only on your device. Location data is never transmitted to any server. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device's Settings.

Export Feature

The app allows you to export your data. When you export, the data is packaged on your device and shared only through the method you choose (AirDrop, email, Files, etc.). We have no involvement in or access to the export process.

4. Third-Party Services

Savor does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not share data with third parties because we do not have access to your data.

5. Children's Privacy

Savor does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

6. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device-level encryption
  • Passcode protection within the app
  • Keychain storage for sensitive credentials
  • No network transmission of personal data

7. Your Rights

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app at all times
  • Export: Use the built-in export feature to get a copy of all your data
  • Delete: Delete individual items within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Portability: Export your data in standard formats at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app, please visit our Support page.

Support

We're here to help you get the most out of Savor.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access premium features?

Long-press the Savor logo on the home screen for 5 seconds. You'll be prompted to enter your passcode to unlock premium features.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. If you uninstall the app, your data will be removed from your device.

How do I export my data?

In the premium section, go to Settings and tap "Export Data." You can export in a human-readable format or as a full backup file. Share it via AirDrop, email, or save to Files.

I forgot my passcode

For security, passcodes cannot be recovered or reset. If you've forgotten your passcode, you will need to reinstall the app. Please use the export feature regularly to back up important data.

Can I change the app icon?

Yes. In premium settings, you can switch between available app icons and display themes.

Does the app use my camera or microphone?

The app requests camera access only if you choose to use photo features. Permissions can be revoked at any time in your device's Settings. No media is ever uploaded or transmitted.
